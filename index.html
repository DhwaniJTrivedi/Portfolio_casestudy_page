Context

Group Project - Design in 5 weeks (May-June 2023)

Problem Statement

Subtrack app was designed to track their all subscriptions at one place by syncing the registered email id. This app also provides Pause feature to save some money on monthly basis and Cost sharing feature to keep user stay in budget.

"How might we improve our online subscription managing service/app so that our users are successful based on reduced time managing their subscriptions, success rate on keep spending within budget and users feedback?"

User Research

Subscription e-commerce: Is a way of purchasing goods and/or services online in which customers pay a recurring fee to receive them on a regular basis.

  • Gym memberships
  • Meal kits
  • Streaming services

This growth is mainly due to companies' stabilizing their output after catering to the demand that grew exponentially during the pandemic.

Due to Subscription e-commerce playing an integral part in how we shop today and its exponential growth rate, we wanted to understand consumer behaviours and pain points around their management.
